What's included

We handle the boring bits.

i.

Full comprehensive insurance

Every booking is covered by our $2m commercial policy. Your guest is bonded for $2,000 of damage cover. You're never out of pocket.

ii.

Guest screening & ID verification

We verify every renter's identity, licence and driving history before they ever see your address. You approve every booking.

iii.

24/7 roadside & guest support

If something breaks on the road, the guest calls us — not you. We have repairers on retainer across NZ.

iv.

Professional photography

One of our local photographers shoots your van so it actually looks like itself. No phone-snap listings.

v.

You stay in control

Block out dates anytime. Set rules. Decide who drives. Stop hosting whenever you want — no contract, no fees to leave.
