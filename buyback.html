How buyback works

Six steps from purchase to sell-back.

Designed for travellers. Built to be fair. Written down before you sign.

01

Pick your van

Browse our buyback fleet. Each is a hand-built conversion that's been on the road with us, fully serviced and ready to go.

02

Agree the terms upfront

You pay the purchase price today. We sign a contract guaranteeing what we'll pay you when you bring it back, between 3 and 12 months later.

03

Drive away — it's yours

The van is fully in your name. You drive it, sleep in it, customise it, freedom-camp in it. We're here if you need us.

04

Hit the road for as long as you want

From three months to a year. Optional rego, WOF and servicing packages available so you don't have to think about it.

05

Bring it back when you're done

Return the van to any of our hubs (Auckland, Wellington, Christchurch, Queenstown). Quick inspection, fair-wear assessed.

06

Get paid that day

Direct credit, same-day clearance. You walk out with the agreed buyback price minus any agreed deductions for damage beyond fair wear.
